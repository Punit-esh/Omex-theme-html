The Future of NoCode

Forbes estimates that the global low-code/NoCode development platform market would generate $187 billion in revenue by 2030. By 2024, it will account for almost 65 percent of all application development activity. Nearly 60% of all custom apps are now created outside of the IT department. The majority of these users (40%) are employed in some capacity. Non-programmers, or basically anyone, may now create business workflow applications using low-code/NoCode tools. Users can now design solutions that reflect specific data queries and workflow procedures, and their low-code/NoCode programmes can be readily linked into bigger business processes.
